Who Am I

I am a software engineer. I have always been very passionate about computer programming. The first computer language that I learned was lego in school and that started my love for computers. While pursuing my Bachelors in Computer Science, I did an internship in a software house in Pakistan and after graduation, I started my first job in the same company as a computer programmer. I worked with a couple of other companies as a software developer and also taught some classes at a college level. After my first child, I decided to take a break and give my full attention to my family and children, but I always missed coding and wanted to come back to my field. When I decided that now was a good time for me to come back and focus on my career, I knew I wanted to modernize my skill set. I heard about App Academy’s full-stack boot camp and I liked their curriculum. In this program, I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript/Node.js, Express, React, Redux, Python, and Flask - to name a few. I am a hard-working team player who learns quickly and can’t wait to continue making an impact in the world of programming.
